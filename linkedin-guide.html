Critical

Контент-план: первые 7 постов для LinkedIn

После упаковки профиля нужно оживить ленту. Один пост в 2-3 дня. Вот темы:

Пост 1 — Intro / Pivot story

Тема: "I used to build no-code apps. Then I realized something..." Hook: Рассказ о переходе от Bubble к AI search optimization CTA: "What's the biggest shift you've made in your career?" Формат: текст, 800-1200 символов

Пост 2 — Case study (Ross Painting)

Тема: "A painting company in San Diego got 6 leads from ChatGPT. Here's how." Hook: Конкретный результат + неожиданный канал Детали: 60 pages, 18 neighborhoods, AI search optimization CTA: "Is your business showing up when people ask ChatGPT for recommendations?" Формат: текст + скриншот (если есть)

Пост 3 — GEO explainer

Тема: "SEO is not dead. But SEO alone is not enough anymore." Hook: 40% of searches now get AI-generated answers Объяснение: что такое GEO, почему бизнесам нужно оптимизировать для AI CTA: Link to geoseo.today Формат: текст, можно carousel

Пост 4 — Building in public

Тема: "I built a 7,500-line audit tool in 2 weeks. Solo. Here's what I learned." Hook: Speed of building with AI tools Детали: tech stack, open source, what Claude/AI helped with CTA: "What are you building with AI?" Формат: текст

Пост 5 — Local SEO tip

Тема: "If you're a local business, here are 3 things your competitors aren't doing" Hook: Конкретный actionable совет 1. Neighborhood-specific landing pages 2. Schema markup for local business 3. Optimizing for AI search citations Формат: numbered list

Пост 6 — Bali founder life

Тема: "Running a US-facing business from Bali. The real pros and cons." Hook: Timezone, culture, cost of living Личное: показать человеческую сторону Формат: текст + фото (опционально)

Пост 7 — Open source announcement

Тема: "I just open-sourced my SEO audit tool. Here's why." Hook: Why free? Business model? What I learned. CTA: "Star it on GitHub / try it free" Формат: текст + link to repo
