history Company Timeline

3 Pivots to Product-Market Fit

The original 2016 idea (voice assistant) only became viable in 2024 when LLMs matured. PMF came from a client request, not from the founder's vision.

2016 — Founded
"Lakshmi" — voice English teacher for workers. High engagement, but "vitamin, not painkiller." Schools didn't pay.
2017 — Pivot #1
Corporate training via WhatsApp. Uber paid Rs 10 lakh for 10K driver training. Revenue ~Rs 50 lakh/year, but contracts didn't scale.
2018 — Pivot #2 (PMF)
Dunzo asked: "Can your bot filter candidate leads?" Simple text bot reduced Dunzo's hiring cost by 30%. Swiggy and Zomato came organically. Product-market fit found.
2019 — Y Combinator S19
At pitch: 1M+ users, 20K placed, 5K DAU. YC validation.
2021 — Series A ($8M)
Led by Khosla Ventures. Scaling to 200+ cities.
2024 — Voice AI + Series B ($10M)
Full circle: Lakshmi 2016 finally works. Deepgram + GPT-4o + ElevenLabs. 20K calls/day. 3x recruiter productivity. Rs 2/min vs Rs 3-4 human.
2025 — Strategic Investors
Persol Group (Japan, #2 HR, $8.7B revenue) + LemmaTree (Temasek). Acquired L.earn (upskilling). Goal: 100K placements/month.

How the Bot Actually Works

chat

Worker Flow (WhatsApp)

  • Step 1: Worker clicks "Apply on WhatsApp" or sends "Hi"
  • Step 2: Bot asks 2 questions (bike? city?) — takes 2 minutes
  • Step 3: Bot answers salary/schedule questions via GPT-4o
  • Step 4: Job confirmed or interview scheduled
  • Step 5: Document verification via Voice AI
  • 50K+ WhatsApp messages/day, 95% open rate
business

Employer Flow (B2B)

  • NOT self-service — works through sales managers
  • Enterprise clients give bulk hiring mandates
  • Mandates distributed to 2,000+ agencies via CRM app
  • No public dashboard, no API, no pricing page
  • Average fulfillment: 2-3 days
  • One client reported 40% cost reduction in 3 months
psychology

Matching Logic — Simpler Than You Think

For 95% of volume (delivery), matching is trivial: bike + license + right city = qualified. The real AI value is elsewhere:

  • Retention Prediction (70% accuracy) — analyzes voice tone, pauses, emotions during calls. "Black box" — even they don't fully understand which signals work
  • Fine-tuned models on Indian accents, dialects, code-switching between languages
  • Training data moat: hundreds of thousands of hours of real recruiter-candidate recordings. Impossible to replicate quickly.

gavel Verdict

Strategic Recommendations

celebration

Entry: Events & Weddings

NOT hotels. Events have urgency ("need tomorrow"), willingness to pay, low entry threshold, and repeat bookings from agencies. Hotels = long sales cycle + BPJS required immediately.

savings

Model: Bootstrap Lifestyle

Target $5-15K MRR. Profitable at 50-100 clients. AllWage (South Africa) proved this: 330 clients, bootstrapped, profitable at $1.70/worker/month. No VC needed.

handshake

First 10: Personal Sales

Via Sukma (local partner), not through the bot. Vahan found PMF from one personal client request (Dunzo). Bot = scaling tool AFTER PMF, not a discovery tool.
